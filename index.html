Dragon Bros Hits PC and Xbox!

Be the Dragon, grab a Gun, destroy Robots, Save your mom!



Dragon Bros is the glue between retro and modern 2D Pixel Art action shooters.
With its colorful graphics and cute dragons with guns, it is aimed at younger audiences as well as nostalgic players.

Gameplay:

Jump and shoot, dodge enemy bullets, swap weapons, collect items to increase your firepower, beware of music sync traps. Fight big bosses at the end of every world.

Features:
  • Local Multiplayer
  • Crazy Run&Gun / Bullet Hell hybrid
  • Adaptive Music levels
  • Excruciating Boss fights
  • Encounters drastically change based on difficulty mode
Release: 31/05/2017
